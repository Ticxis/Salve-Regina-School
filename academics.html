Academics at Salve Regina School

Our innovative curriculum and dedicated staff empower learners to excel in a dynamic, global world through rigorous academics and creative learning opportunities.

Explore our diverse range of programmes designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and leadership in every learner.

STEM

Our STEM programme emphasizes science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, preparing learners for cutting-edge careers.
