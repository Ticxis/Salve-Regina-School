About Us



Salve Regina School is a premier private school dedicated to fostering academic excellence, personal growth, and global citizenship. Founded in 1985, our institution combines rigorous academics with a nurturing environment.

Our vision is to provide top-notch education that builds into the creation, in our learners, first class global citizenship with excellent skills and confidence.
