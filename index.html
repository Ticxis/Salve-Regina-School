About Us



Salve Regina School is a private pre-tertiary education institution in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. Established in 2018 with just five children, we now have over 550 learners from the pre-school to junior high level.


With our world-class facilities and quality teaching and learning, Salve Regina is among the most sought-after schools in the Region. Situated in the vibrant hills of Abuakwa-Canaan, Kumasi, the school is dedicated to equipping young minds with the tools they need for a bright future.


Our vision is to provide top-notch education that builds into the creation, in our learners, first class global citizenship with excellent skills and confidence.
