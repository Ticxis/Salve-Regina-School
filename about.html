About Us

Salve Regina School is a private pre-tertiary education institution in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. Established in 2018 with just five children, we now have our 550 pupils and students from the pre-school to junior high levels. With our world-class facilities and quality teaching and learning, Salve Regina is among the most sought-after schools in the Region.


Situated in the vibrant hills of Abuakwa-Canan, Kumasi, the School is dedicated to equipping young minds with the tools they need for a bright future. Guided by our motto, "Knowledge and Morality", we strive to provide an education that balances academic excellence with strong moral values.


At Salve Regina School, we foster a nurturing and inclusive environment that inspires learners to reach their full potential. Our dedicated educators, state-of-the-art facilities, and emphasis on character development ensure that every learner is prepared to succeed and make a positive impact in the world.
