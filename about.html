About Salve Regina School

At Salve Regina School, we are dedicated to fostering a nurturing environment where students develop intellectually, socially, and emotionally to become global leaders and compassionate innovators.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide top-notch education that builds into the creation, in our learners, first class global citizenship with excellent skills and confidence.


Our Mission


Academic Excellence

Recruit and retain top-notch professional staff for the benefit of our learners.

Inclusive Community

Provide conducive and welcoming environment that enables top of the range teaching and learning experience for our learners.

Innovative Learning

Provide opportunities for healthy lifestyle and discipline through sports and games, cadet corps and other co-curricular activities.


Our Core Values


Knowledge

We nurture knowledge through rigorous academics, diverse perspectives, and a passion for discovery.

Morality

We instill moral values to develop compassionate, principled individuals who uphold fairness and make a difference in the world.

Our History

Founded in 1960, Salve Regina School has grown from a small community school to a leading educational institution. For over six decades, we have been committed to excellence, nurturing generations of students who have become leaders, scientists, and artists.

  • 1960: Salve Regina School was established with a vision to provide top-notch education.
  • 1985: Expanded our campus to include state-of-the-art science labs and a library.
  • 2000: Received national accreditation for academic excellence.
  • 2015: Launched our STEM program, fostering innovation in science and technology.
  • 2020: Celebrated 60 years of shaping futures with a global alumni network.
