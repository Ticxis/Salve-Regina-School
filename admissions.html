Admission at Salve Regina School

Begin your journey with us at Salve Regina School, where we welcome students and families to discover exceptional educational opportunities. Our streamlined admissions process is designed to help you find your place in our vibrant learning community.

Welcome to Our Admissions Process

At our school, we are committed to fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment where every student can thrive. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent and welcoming, ensuring that prospective students and their families feel supported every step of the way. We value diversity, creativity, and academic curiosity, and we look forward to getting to know you and your aspirations.

Our curriculum is tailored to challenge and inspire students, preparing them for success in an ever-changing world. From our dedicated faculty to our state-of-the-art facilities, we provide a holistic education that balances academics, extracurricular activities, and personal growth. Whether you're interested in STEM, the arts, or athletics, our school offers opportunities to explore your passions and develop your talents.

To apply, we invite you to explore our admissions requirements and deadlines, which are outlined in detail on this page. Our admissions team is here to answer any questions and guide you through the process. We can't wait to welcome you to our school family!

Why Choose Our School?

"Our school is more than just a place to learn—it's a community where students grow, connect, and discover their potential. With a focus on personalized education, we ensure that every student receives the support they need to succeed. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, and our dedicated staff are passionate about helping students achieve their goals. Join us and become part of a legacy of excellence and opportunity."
- Salve Regina School Administration

Schedule an Introductory Call

We welcome learning about your student and your family's interest in Salve Regina during an introductory conversation with a member of our Admissions team! This conversation also presents an ideal opportunity to highlight our application process for you.

Visit Campus

Experience our vibrant community firsthand by scheduling a campus visit. During your visit, you'll tour our facilities, meet with faculty members, observe classes in session, and get a feel for daily life at Salve Regina School. Our student ambassadors will be happy to answer your questions and share their experiences.

Complete Parent Application

Following your introductory call, a member of the Admissions team will send you form. This is where you can complete the Parent Application with detailed information about your family and student's academic background.

Request and Submit Materials

Gather and submit all required documentation including academic transcripts, teacher recommendations or any material specific to your student's grade level. Our admissions team will provide you with a detailed checklist and deadlines for all required materials.

Entrance Exam

Applicants will be required to take an assessment exam to determine which level he/she will be admitted into.

Explore Your Financial Options

Learn about our financial aid opportunities, scholarship programmes and flexible payment plans. Our financial aid team will work with you to understand the investment in your child's education and explore options that make Salve Regina School accessible for your family.

Schedule Introductory Call

We welcome learning about your student and your family's interest in Salve Regina during an introductory conversation with a member of our Admissions team! Submit an inquiry form, and our team will be in touch shortly to schedule your call.

Visit Campus

Experience our vibrant community firsthand by scheduling a campus visit. Tour our facilities, meet faculty members, observe classes, and get a feel for daily life at Salve Regina School.

Complete Parent Application

Following your introductory call, a member of the Admissions team will send you credentials to access our online application portal where you can complete the Parent Application.

Request and Submit Materials

Gather and submit all required documentation including academic transcripts, teacher recommendations, and any additional materials specific to your student's grade level.

Register for Group Visit/Entrance Exam

Applicants for grades 4-5 attend a half-day Group Visit. Applicants for grades 6-9 submit ISEE or SSAT scores. Our team will guide you through the appropriate requirements.
